Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 28-Jul-2025

Last Updated: 28-Jul-2025

Company Name: Save The Date


Welcome to Save The Date, a wedding planning platform and mobile app designed to connect couples with wedding service providers. We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your data when you use our services.

1. Who We Are

  • Save The Date operates a dual-platform system: Viewer Accounts: Free accounts for users planning a wedding.
  • Supplier Accounts: Paid accounts for wedding industry professionals to showcase services.
  • Our platform includes a mobile app and web portal, featuring tools like guest list management, appointment scheduling, notes, and a vision board.

2. What Information We Collect

A. From Viewers

  • Name
  • Email Address
  • Mobile Number
  • Wedding Planning Data (e.g. guest list, appointments, notes, vision board content)

B. From Suppliers

  • Business Name
  • Contact Person Details
  • Email & Phone Number
  • Business Description & Services
  • Media Content (photos, videos, logos)
  • Payment & Billing Information

C. Automatically Collected Data

  • Device information
  • IP address
  • Location data (if enabled)
  • Usage logs and interaction data

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and maintain the app’s services and tools.
  • Allow viewers to browse, contact, and book suppliers.
  • Enable suppliers to manage and publish their business profile.
  • Process payments for suppliers.
  • Send system updates, promotions, and notifications.
  • Analyze trends and improve user experience.
  • Prevent fraud and enforce platform policies.

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data. All sensitive data (like passwords and payment details) is encrypted and securely stored.


6. User Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access and review your personal data.
  • Correct or update your information.
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data.
  • Withdraw consent for data processing (may affect functionality).
  • You can make these requests by contacting us at Help@SaveDate.com.au

7. Third-Party Services

ome features may involve third-party integrations (e.g., payment gateways, cloud storage). These services have their own privacy policies and we recommend reviewing them.


8. Cookies & Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies to enhance user experience and collect usage data. You can disable cookies through your device settings, but some features may not work properly.


9. Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for children under 16. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors.


10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes via email or in-app notifications.
